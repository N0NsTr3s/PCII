JD Vance says giving US economic interest in Ukraine is a security guarantee
Vice President JD Vance stated that giving the U.S. An economic interest in Ukraine serves as a security guarantee for the country invaded by Russia in February 2022.
President Donald Trump is pursuing a minerals deal with Ukraine, which has valuable lithium deposits, as part of the U.S. Aid to Ukraine during the conflict.
Vance emphasized that economic investment in Ukraine is a superior security guarantee compared to military troops from countries lacking recent combat experience.
Vance emphasized that this deal would demonstrate to Ukrainians that America is committed to their sovereignty and will aid in rebuilding the country.
